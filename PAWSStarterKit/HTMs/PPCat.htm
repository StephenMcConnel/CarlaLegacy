Pre/Post-Positional Phrases - Category


You need to make sure the pre/post-positions in your lexicon will be mapped to the syntax category P. Choose one of the following options, depending on your situation:

  • If you already have pre/post-positions marked with the category P in your lexicon nothing more needs to be done.
  • If you have pre/postpositions marked in your lexicon with a distinct category all their own, but it is different from P, go to the Category Mapper and map your morphological category to P.
  • If pre/post-positions are mixed in with another category in the lexicon, you need to add the feature P to each appropriate entry so the category will be changed.
