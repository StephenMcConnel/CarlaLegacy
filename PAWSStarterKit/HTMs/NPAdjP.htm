Nominal Phrases - AdjP Modifiers

Adjective phrases (AdjP) come next, discussed earlier in section 4 Adjectives and AdjP. This means we will be able to parse nominal phrases such as these in English:


1. just those very few old, dirty books.
2. all the boy's many black dogs.
3. only the first one hundred very eager young boys.

As the English examples show, more than one adjective phrase is allowed before the noun. This will be handled in the syntactic parser by a recursive rule.
