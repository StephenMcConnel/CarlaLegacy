Nominal Phrase - Noun Case

Is case morphologically marked on nouns?
No, case is not marked at all, or only marked on the pronouns
Yes
  
Which system of case marking is used?
Nominative-accusative (where all subjects are nominative case and direct objects are accusative case)
Ergative-absolutive (where subjects of transitive verbs are ergative case and subjects of intransitive verbs and objects of transitive verbs are absolutive case)

In the lexicon, mark the affix(es) that label nominative case with the feature nominative, along with any other agreement features they also mark. Add the feature accusative to the affixes that mark accusative case.

In the lexicon, mark the affix(es) that label ergative case with the feature ergative, along with any other agreement features they also mark. Add the feature absolutive to the affixes that mark absolutive case.
