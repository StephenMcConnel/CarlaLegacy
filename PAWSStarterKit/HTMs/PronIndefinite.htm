Pronouns and Proper Names - Indefinite Pronouns

A final type of pronouns is indefinite pronouns. In English, these include everybody, everything, everyone, anybody, something, etc. Also, nobody, nothing, none are negative versions of the indefinite pronouns. Note that these are made up of a quantifier or negative plus an indefinite generic noun, and some languages may not merge them into a single word, as in no one for English.
