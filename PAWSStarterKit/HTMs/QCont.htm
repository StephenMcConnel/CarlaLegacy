Questions - Content Questions

Content questions differ from Yes/No questions in that they contain an interrogative word or phrase, known as wh-phrases in English because they almost all begin with wh (who, what, why, where, when, how, which).

Some examples of English main clause content questions are:


1. Which boy hit the ball?
2. Who hit the ball?
3. Who might have hit the ball?
4. What did the boy hit?
5. Where is the leader?
6. When will she come?
