Pre/Post-Positional Phrases - Complements

Prepositions or postpositions normally take a nominal phrase for their complements, as in to Bill's house and under the giant Sycamore tree. In addition, some pre/post-positions can stand alone, such as in the sentences Come right in, or Turn the light on/off.
