Adverbs and AdvP - Manner Adverbs

Manner adverbs directly modify the verb and usually end in -ly in English. They do not seem to have clear counterparts of other phrase types, except possibly some comparatives, such as as fast as he could. In English, manner adverbs can be modified by very and so, as in:


1. very quickly.
2. so slowly.
3. very well.
