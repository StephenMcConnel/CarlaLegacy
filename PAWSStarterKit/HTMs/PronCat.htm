Pronouns and Proper Names - Category


You need to make sure the pronouns in your lexicon will be mapped to the syntax category Pron. Choose one of the following options, depending on your situation:

  • If you already have pronouns marked with the category Pron in your lexicon nothing more needs to be done.
  • If you have pronouns marked in your lexicon with a distinct category all their own, but it is different from Pron, go to the Category Mapper and map your morphological category to Pron.
