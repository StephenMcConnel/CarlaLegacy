Nominal Phrases - Articles and Demonstratives

Now consider articles and demonstratives. In English, the articles are a, an, and the. English demostratives include this, that, these, and those. Some simple examples are:


1. a book.
2. an apple.
3. the book.
4. the books.
5. this apple.
6. those books.

In English, articles and demonstratives do not co-occur in the same nominal phrase, and both occur before the noun. They may be able to co-occur in your language, or they may occur in different positions with respect to the noun. Therefore, we will give them different syntactic categories.
