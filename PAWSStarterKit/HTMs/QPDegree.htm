Quantifiers and QP - Degree Words

Other quantifiers, like many, much, and few, come in a different position in the nominal phrase. In English, this is after an article, demonstrative or possessor and before an adjective and noun (as in the examples above). Further, these quantifiers can be modified by degree words such as very and so, as in very many dogs or so much waste.
