Adjectives and AdjP - Modifiers

Here we need to consider again what types of words can modify qualitative adjectives themselves.


   Some English examples are:
1. very big
2. so blue
3. extremely large
4. very well built
5. very easily forgotten

Not all combinations of words work due to semantics and other considerations, but in general it seems that certain degree words and manner adverbs or manner adverb phrases can modify adjectives.
