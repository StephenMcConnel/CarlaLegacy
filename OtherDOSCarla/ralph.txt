RALPH.TXT Release notes for RALPH Right Alphabetization

RALPH alphabetizes lines from the right to group similar suffixes.
RALPH is documented in the book "AMPLE: A Tool for Exploring
Morphology".

Change history:

1.1a Apr 93 fixes a bug which removed the top bit from upper
ASCII characters.

1.1b Sep 1998 Created DJGPP, Win32, and 8086 versions.
